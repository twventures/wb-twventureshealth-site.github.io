TW Ventures Inc. Group Benefits Plan

To be eligible for the TW Ventures Inc. Group Benefits Plan for Tier 3 employees, you must:

  • Work for a Participating Employer
  • Not be covered under a collective bargaining agreement
  • Be paid by Cast & Crew, Entertainment Partners or CAPS and, you are either:
    • A full-time employee scheduled to work 30 or more hours per week:
    • A variable employee and you work or are credited as working at least 1,560 hours
